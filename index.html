About

The Committee on Student Fees and Budget Review (CSF) is a Chancellor’s advisory committee composed of undergraduate and graduate students at the University of California, Berkeley. CSF works closely with campus executive leadership, management, and staff to recommend on the management and allocation of a $33 million Student Services Fee Fund.
